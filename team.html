Focus Areas

Our interdisciplinary work spans multiple domains in AI and education

Developing teams of specialized AI agents that work together to provide personalized learning experiences and educational support.

Educational Technology & Learning Analytics

Building metrics and methodologies to measure the effectiveness of AI-powered educational interventions on student learning outcomes.

Entrepreneurship Education

Creating AI systems that accelerate entrepreneurship learning by providing real-time coaching and feedback throughout the startup development process.

Human-AI Interaction

Designing how students interact with AI tutoring systems and creating interfaces that promote effective learning partnerships between humans and AI.
